How It Works

From documents to answers.

An AI knowledge base connects your existing company documents to a private assistant your team can search instantly.

1. Upload your documents

Connect SOPs, FAQs, policies, training docs, PDFs, and any internal knowledge your team relies on.

2. Knowledge is indexed

The system processes your documents into a vector database — a meaning-aware search index that retrieves content by topic, not just exact keywords.

3. Employees ask questions

Your team types questions in plain English — the same way they'd ask a colleague. No special syntax needed.

4. AI answers from approved sources only

The assistant retrieves from your uploaded documents only and generates a clear answer. It does not draw on general internet knowledge.

5. Every answer includes citations

Responses include the source document name and page number so your team can verify any answer directly.

Gaps are surfaced automatically

When no documentation covers a question, the system flags it — helping you identify and close knowledge gaps over time.

Technical note

This approach is called RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation). Instead of relying on an AI's general training data, RAG retrieves relevant content from your company documents first, then generates an answer grounded in that source material. We use a vector database — a search index that finds content based on meaning rather than exact keyword matches — to make retrieval fast and accurate even across large document libraries.
