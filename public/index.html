What It Is

What is an AI knowledge base?

An AI knowledge base is a private search assistant for your business. Instead of manually digging through documents, your team can ask questions in natural language and get answers based on your approved company knowledge.

Upload or connect your documents

SOPs, FAQs, policies, training docs, PDFs — any text your team relies on.

Ask questions in plain English

Your team types a question the same way they'd ask a colleague. No special syntax needed.

Get answers with sources

The assistant returns a clear answer and shows which document it came from.

How It Works

What is RAG?

RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation. It means the AI searches your company documents before answering. Instead of guessing from general training data, the assistant retrieves the most relevant information, writes a clear answer, and shows the source.
