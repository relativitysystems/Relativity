How It Works

From documents to answers.

An AI knowledge base connects your existing company documents to a private assistant your team can search instantly.

1. Upload your documents

Connect SOPs, FAQs, policies, training guides, and any internal documents your team relies on.

2. Relativity indexes your knowledge

Your documents are processed and stored in a private vector database. The AI learns the meaning behind your content, not just keywords.

3. Employees ask questions

Your team asks in plain English. No special training or syntax required.

4. AI answers from approved sources

The assistant retrieves answers only from your uploaded documents and includes the exact source and page number in every response.

5. Gaps are surfaced automatically

When a question has no documented answer, the system flags it clearly so you know exactly what to document next.

Technical note

Relativity uses retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) — a technique that connects an AI language model to your specific documents instead of the open internet. Your knowledge base is private, isolated, and only answers from what you've approved. A vector database makes retrieval fast and accurate even across large document libraries by searching by meaning, not just exact keywords.
